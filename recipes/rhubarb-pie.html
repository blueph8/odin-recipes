Description

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups chopped rhubarb
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 6 Tablespoon flour
  • 1 Tablespoon butter
  • Butter Crust

Optional - to taste

  • cinnamon
  • nutmeg
  • eggs

Steps:

  1. 1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F.
  2. 2. Combine sugar and flour. Sprinkle 1/4 of it over pastry in pie plate.
  3. 3. Heap rhubarb over this mixture. Sprinkle with remaining sugar and flour. Dot with butter.
  4. 4. Cover with top crust, place pie on lowest rack, and bake for 15 minutes.
  5. 5. Reduce oven temperature to 350 degrees F and continue baking for 45 minutes.
  6. 6. Fin. Serve Warm or Cold or with Whipping Cream.
