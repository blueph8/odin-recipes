Whipped Cream

  • 1 Cup Heavy Cream
  • 1 Tablespoon Baker's Sugar
  • Pinch of Salt
  1. 1. Freeze the bowl to use 30 min prior.
  2. 2. Get out a stand mixer or a whisk.
  3. 3. Start whisking on the lowest setting, for about 3-4 minutes.
  4. 4. At soft whip, add sugar and salt slowly while mixer on. Continue for another 30 sec - 1 min.
  5. 5. Stop at hard peaks. Unless you want sweetened butter.
